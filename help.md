# Salary Analysis — kode24 2025

## About the data

The data comes from **kode24**'s annual salary survey for Norwegian software developers and IT professionals. The 2025 survey collected self-reported salary figures along with background information such as field of work, location, job type, gender, years of education, and years of experience.

The dataset contains roughly 1,987 cleaned entries (outliers removed: salaries outside 100 000–5 000 000 NOK, and implausible education years capped at 20).

## Data source

- **Article**: [Last ned kode24s lønnstall for 2025 og lag noe sjæl!](https://www.kode24.no/artikkel/last-ned-kode24s-lonnstall-for-2025-og-lag-noe-sjaelv/244319)
- **Direct download**: The JSON file is fetched automatically on first run from kode24's file server.

## How to use

1. **Filters** (left sidebar): narrow down the dataset by location, job type, education range, and experience range.
2. **My Profile** (left sidebar): enter your own salary and background to see where you stand relative to the filtered group.
3. **Charts tab**: salary distribution histogram, salary by field (IQR bars + median), and experience vs. salary scatter plot — all updated live as you change filters or your salary.
4. **Table tab**: full tabular view of filtered entries, sortable and searchable per column.
